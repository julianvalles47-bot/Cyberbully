Helpful stuff

things you can do if you need them

What to do right now ▼
take a breath. you are gonna be okay.
screenshot everything as proof
block the person if you can
tell someone you trust what happened
save those screenshots somewhere safe
How to block or report someone ▼
open their message or profile
tap the options menu (three dots)
click block or report
follow what the app says
save screenshots first just in case
Saving proof (screenshots) ▼
screenshot the mean messages
make sure username and date are visible
save to a private folder or cloud
dont edit or crop them
only show to adults you trust
What to say (and what not to) ▼
DO: stay calm and factual
DO: save proof first
DONT: blow up at them
DONT: fight back the same way
DONT: post it publicly
When to tell an adult ▼
if you feel unsafe
if it keeps happening
if someone else is getting hit too
if its messing with your head
you dont have to handle this alone
Protecting your accounts ▼
make your profiles private
turn on two factor authentication
use different passwords
check your followers and friends
dont post personal stuff publicly
Stuff to know

just some info in case its helpful

ℹ

What is cyberbullying

basically when someone is being a jerk online threats, spreading rumors, humiliation. can happen on social media, games, texts.

♥

How it affects people

it messes with your head. anxiety, feeling bad about yourself, depressed. its not your fault and you didnt deserve it.

👥

How to get help

talk to someone. us, a teacher, your parents, counselor. just dont bottle it up.

📞

Who to reach out to

school counselor is a good start. parents if you can. also the platform itself has report buttons.
